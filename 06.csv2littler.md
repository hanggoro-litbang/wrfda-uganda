# Convert CSV file to LittleR format
1. download the zip file and extract https://github.com/hanggoro-litbang/wrfda-uganda/blob/main/csv2littleR.zip to your local folder
2. Upload to your google drive
3. Right click on your **csv2littleR_combine.ipynb** and open with google collab
4. Check on PARAMETER section and edit the location and cycle
5. Run cell by cell
6. Check the output on your google drive (grobs_sinop.2023082312 and grobs_temp.2023082312)
